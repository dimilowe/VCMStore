⭐ SECTION 5 — Frequently Asked Questions (SEO Gold)
What is Prediction Center?
Prediction Center is a free forecasting tool where anyone can submit predictions and vote Yes/No on future events.
Is this gambling or betting?
No. There is no money involved, no trading, and no payouts.
It is strictly for fun, curiosity, and community forecasting.
Do I need an account to vote?
No. Prediction Center uses anonymous cookies so each user gets one vote per prediction.
How do predictions get resolved?
When the outcome becomes publicly verifiable, the prediction is marked as Resolved: YES or Resolved: NO.
Can anyone submit a prediction?
Yes — anyone can submit a future question as long as it is clear and objectively resolvable.
Does Prediction Center use AI?
Not for voting — results are entirely based on community sentiment.
AI may be used to improve moderation and insights.
Is Prediction Center accurate?
Accuracy depends on how informed the community is.
The more people vote, the more reliable the percentages become.
Is the tool free?
Yes. Prediction Center is completely free to use.
